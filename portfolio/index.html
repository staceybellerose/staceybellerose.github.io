Random Word Generator


The Random Word Generator Android app generates randomly selected words from a large English (American) dictionary of over 28,000 entries. By using a large dictionary, a randomly selected word will have nearly 14.8 bits of entropy. A phrase made up of 6 words will have over 88.6 bits of entropy, more than enough for a very strong password.
